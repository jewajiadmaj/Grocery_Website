About us

"Always give your customer the quality and service they deserve"

These were the words that have always been our foundation at Jewaji Adamji. From the time it all started, five generations prior, we have stuck to this saying and will always follow this in the future as well. Because for us, customer always comes first.

Our shop was started years ago by Jewaji, the namesake of the shop, in the market of Dhanmandi, Udaipur. A small shop with the dream of serving the best quality materials to our customer and expnading and growing the shop. And the journey started from there. With time and constant efforts we built trust with customers by giving them the best quality of products and service.The shop was passed to his son, Saifuddin Ji, and later to his sons, Siraj and Firoz Ji, and with each geneartion the shop expanded. And we built up from a small shop to what we are today.

Today we have two shops in the two of the major grocery market in Udaipur. In Dhanmandi, we have a retail shop to provide all kinds of products that you need in your day-to-day life. And the second shop is in Krishimandi, where we deal in wholesale. We provide you wholesale products including Wheat, rice, spices, dry fruits and all types of grocery items. Currently we are also serving restaurants, cafes and hotels in Udaipur including some of the well known names such as Meera Restaurant, Sangria Cafe, Hotel Mewari Villa and Jalsa Restaurant. We also provide our services to caterers including the well known Nakoda caterers, Agarwal Caterers, Dalchand Caterers ,Virat caterers, Vishal caterers, Hazareshwer caterers and many more as well as to companies such as Wooden Streets and Cognus Technologies.

Our services can now also be availed sitting at home, by using our newly launched website. All you have to do it pick your products and they will be delivered to your doorsteps.

We will keep on serving our customer with the quality and service they deserve but also keep on improving to make the experience even better and easier.
